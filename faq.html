Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tantric Yoga?

Tantric Yoga is a transformative practice that integrates movement, breathwork, meditation, and self-awareness. Dr. C’s approach blends ancient Tantric wisdom with modern techniques for holistic well-being.

How do I book a session?

Visit the Get Started page to book a session via Calendly or send a message through the contact form.

Are sessions available online?

Yes! All services are available online, with in-person options in San Francisco, CA by request.

The package includes all Tantric Yoga sessions, bi-weekly coaching calls, exclusive resources, and personalized support from Dr. C.

Can beginners join?

Absolutely. All levels are welcome, and Dr. C tailors sessions to each individual’s needs and experience.
